WHO WE ARE?

DoubleMorgan is an advisory and consulting company that specializes in helping future technology companies (Blockchain & Crypto, Metaverse, FinTech, and AI) to meet Investors under one roof for them to be able to create long-term business relationships.

WHY DO INVESTORS ATTEND?

DoubleMorgan attracts investors from across Dubai and the Middle East. Investors attend to find investment opportunities in future technology companies (Blockchain & Crypto, Metaverse, FinTech, and AI) & startups with a goal to find the next "Unicorn".

WHY COMPANIES ATTEND?

Major strategic partners and Fortune 500 companies attend to gain education about the future technology Industry which is changing rapidly. They attend with clear goals to meet the top startups & companies in the space and create long-term partnerships with them.

WHY DOUBLEMORGAN?

The DoubleMorgan currently holds the position of being the most focused investment advisory company for the future technology industry (Blockchain & Crypto, Metaverse, FinTech, and AI). This means that DoubleMorgan focuses on high-quality investors and strategic partners. This is extremely important as it makes sure all parties in participation with the expectations of doing business with one another.

WHAT IS OUR MISSION?

Our mission has two parts. First and foremost, educating the public on future technologies and how all the elements that make it up (Blockchain & Crypto, Metaverse, FinTech, and AI) will completely change the way we interact in the coming years is a priority for DoubleMorgan. Secondly our mission is to make sure that Founders, Investors, and members of the future technology industry (Blockchain & Crypto, Metaverse, FinTech, and AI) world have a dedicated place to meet and interact in order to create valuable business relationships.

WHY DUBAI?

DoubleMorgan and its holding company have been in the UAE for more than a decade. And making Dubai the base of operations has worked to be our greatest investment.
